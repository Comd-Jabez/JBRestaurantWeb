About us

Our lovely establishment was founded in 2014 with one dream, to bring families together. Our founder Johnny, being a father himself has always said that the reasons that he founded Daddiess Patties was for "bring a father and his kid to a place where they can eat and bond" Something that he wished he had while he was growing up. Of course, since we have been around this long, Johnny believes that his actions have not been in vain.

The founder? Daddy Johnny of course

You'll find yourself seated at one of our many fine dining tables, we're rated best in terms of presentation.
